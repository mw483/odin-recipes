Odin Recipes

A list of my favorite recipes!

Atsuage kimchi stir-fry

Atsuage is a pre-fried tofu sold in Japan, shaped like blocks. Stir-frying with tofu makes it flavorful! A healthy, cheap, and completely vegan dish!

Dry curry

A style of Japanese curry but made with less water, and uses minced meat and mixed veggies instead of the usual diced meat, carrots, and potatoes.

Spaghetti Bolognese

Basic spaghetti with meat and tomato sauce, with ingredients easily accessible in Japan
